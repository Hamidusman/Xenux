About Xenux

Writing you resume is hard, typing one that's ATS compatible? Even harder. But worry not, because that's what im here to solve

Xenux is a simple but efficient resume builder designed to support job candidates with a resume that would bypass that pesky ATS software when applying for jobs online.

But What Is ATS?
Application Tracking Software is a software application used by employers to manage the hiring process, especially in the initial stages of recruitment. The primary purpose of an ATS is to automate and streamline the sorting, scanning, and handling of job applications.
